Bio

Hey there! I'm Amol 👋

I'm a self-driven web developer from India with a passion for building clean, fast, and visually engaging web experiences. I enjoy the full spectrum of frontend development — from crafting pixel-perfect layouts to connecting them to robust backends.

When I'm not shipping code, you'll find me live on YouTube — streaming everything from high-intensity FPS games to laid-back story-driven adventures. Gaming has always been a creative outlet for me, and sharing that with a community makes it even better.

I believe great work happens at the intersection of craft and passion. Whether it's a polished UI or a clutch play, I always aim to bring energy and intentionality to everything I create.
